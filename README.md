#My--AI--idea
AI for fair distribution of food and medicine

AI for Social Good: Resource Distribution
​Project Goal
​The goal of this project is to use Artificial Intelligence to distribute surplus food and medicine to people in need, reducing waste and supporting the community.

​Technical Details
​AI Technique: We use Linear Regression to predict which areas will have the highest demand for specific resources.
​Data Sources: The system analyzes local community needs, current inventory levels, and expiration dates to optimize delivery.
​Impact
​This AI solution helps ensure that vital resources reach people before they expire, promoting social justice and fair acces to essentials.
